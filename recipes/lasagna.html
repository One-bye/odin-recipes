Lasagna Bolognese

Description:

Lasagna Bolognese is an extremely delicious dish that is prepared in layers of pasta. We prepared these sauces together with the previous recipes, so you already have them at hand.

For a successful dish, you must first of all use quality ingredients, I know I've always said this, but it's extremely important.

Ingredients:

    For Bolognese sauce:

  • 1 kg minced beef
  • 1 kg of mashed tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 carrots and 2 onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • a little rosemary
  • Italian herbs
  • salt and pepper

    For the bechamel sauce:

  • 700 ml of milk
  • 70 g flour and 70 g of butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • a little thyme
  • nutmeg
  • pepper and salt

    For assembly:

  • 200 g grated mozzarella
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Chop the vegetables in the robot
  2. Cook the vegetables and meat in a pot with oil
  3. After the meat is cooked, we will add the tomato paste
  4. Cook the bechamel sauce and add it to a pan greased with butter in which we will assemble the lasagna
  5. After assembling the lasagna, add it to the oven at 180 degrees for an hour
